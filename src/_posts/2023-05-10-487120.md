Oyama had a long career at NASA, during which he served as Life Detection Systems branch chief; he [examined lunar soil samples](https://youtu.be/Vhx01meKsCs) and also helped design experiments for the [Viking Mars Landers](https://mars.nasa.gov/mars-exploration/missions/viking-1-2/). Both he and his brother [Jiro](https://history.nasa.gov/SP-4314/Chap2.pdf) pioneered new areas of life sciences research at Ames.
